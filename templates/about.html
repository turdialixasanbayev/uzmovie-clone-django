About Us

Uzmovi Clone is a movie streaming platform built for demonstration purposes. Our mission is to provide users with the best movie browsing experience, including the latest releases, popular titles, and user-curated wishlists.

This project is a learning clone of the original uzmovi.tv website, developed using Django and Bootstrap.

Here you can explore movies by categories, watch trailers, and save your favorite titles in your personal wishlist.

Enjoy your stay!
